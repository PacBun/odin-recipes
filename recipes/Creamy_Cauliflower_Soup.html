Creamy Cauliflower Soup


Description

Ingredients

  • Onion 80 g
  • Cauliflower 400 g
  • Potato 200 g
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Olive Oil 2 tbsp + for serving
  • Milk 250 ml
  • Vegetable Stock 5000 ml
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley for serving

Steps

  1. Chop Cauliflower in pieces and drip the olive oil and Garlic. Bake it in the oven with 250F.
  2. Boil the potato with milk and veggie stock. Add baked Cauliflower and bend it smoothly.
  3. Add pepper and salt in final. Serve and Top up with chopped Parsley
