Kimchi Mac n Cheese


Description

Ingredients

  • Chifferi Rigate 220 g
  • Salt
  • Butter45 g
  • All-purpose Flour3 tbsp
  • Milk550 ml
  • Mozzarella Cheese100 + 150 g
  • Kimchi160 g
  • Gochujang2 tsp
  • Gochugaru1 tsp
  • Salt and Pepper for serving
  • Olive oil for serving

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in a pot of salted boiling water until al dente, strain and rinse through cold water.
  2. Preheat the oven to 230Â°C.
  3. Melt butter in a pan, add flour and stir until flour is cooked, but still retain light yellow color. Gradually add milk and stir until smooth. Simmer the sauce until thickened, about 3 minutes. Add 100 g of cheese and stir until homogenous.
  4. Add gochujang, gochugaru, and kimchi. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  5. Add pasta and stir through the sauce. Transfer pasta into a baking dish, top with mozzarella cheese and broil in the oven until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
  6. Drizzle with a touch of olive oil and serve immediately.
