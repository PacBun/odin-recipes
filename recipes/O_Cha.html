O-Cha


Description

Ingredients

  • Thai Tea Powder 2 tsp
  • Hot Water 45 ml
  • Condensed Milk 30 g
  • Milk 60 ml
  • Condensed Milk 30 g
  • Espresso 1 shot
  • Ice

Steps

  1. For Thai Tea: Steep Thai tea in hot water for 5 minutes and strain. Flavour the tea with condensed milk and set aside to cool.
  2. For Sweet Milk: Mix milk and condensed milk until combined.
  3. Prepare espresso shot from your favorite capsule or instant coffee.
  4. Add Thai tea into a serving glass, followed by ice, sweet milk, and espresso shot.
